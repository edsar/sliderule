DATASET/QUESTIONS

The datasets for sales, violations, and businesses (applicants for marijuana business licenses)

Research questions: (1) What is relationships between sales and violations? (2) What are the predictors of violations? (3) How can we segment the businesses (identified in applicants data) by risk (likelihood of violating)?
